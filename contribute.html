Contribute

The Larkspur Community Foundation has agreed to assist with fundraising efforts to upgrade Heatherwood Park as our objectives align with their mission. Credit card donations can be made via the Larkspur Community Foundation's PayPal (link to: https://mobile.paypal.com/us/cgi-bin/webscr?cmd=_express-checkout-mobile&useraction=commit&token=EC-08K95153H3925152C) Please mail check donations to: Larkspur Community Foundation PO Box 43 Larkspur, CA 94977-0043 > Donations are tax deductible per guidelines for contributions to charitable organizations > Funds will be used exclusively to upgrade Heatherwood Park > Appreciated securities donations may be accepted Please note "Helping Heatherwood Park" in the notation! The Larkspur Community Foundation has been organized to encourage philanthropy within and for the City of Larkspur, California. It will support projects and activities that sustain the City’s historic small-town character and enhance the community’s quality of life for all generations, now and in the future. To donate appreciated securities, please contact the Larkspur Community Foundation Please contact AJ Rea with any questions about support for Heatherwood Park.
