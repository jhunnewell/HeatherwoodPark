Contribute

The Larkspur Community Foundation has agreed to assist with fundraising efforts to upgrade Heatherwood Park as our objectives align with their mission.
Credit card donations can be made via the Larkspur Community Foundation's PayPal account:

Please note "Helping Heatherwood Park" in the notation!

Please mail check donations to:
Larkspur Community Foundation
PO Box 43
Larkspur, CA 94977-0043

  • Donations are tax deductible per guidelines for contributions to charitable organizations
  • Funds will be used exclusively to upgrade Heatherwood Park
  • Appreciated securities donations may be accepted

    • The Larkspur Community Foundation has been organized to encourage philanthropy within and for the City of Larkspur, California. It will support projects and activities that sustain the City’s historic small-town character and enhance the community’s quality of life for all generations, now and in the future.
    To donate appreciated securities, please contact the Larkspur Community Foundation larkspurcommunityfoundation.org

    Please contact AJ Rea with any questions about support for Heatherwood Park.
