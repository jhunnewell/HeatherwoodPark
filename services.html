Parlor's Signature

A customized, results-driven facial for every skin. Features gentle enzymes, antioxidants and deep skin renewal with plant-infused products, leaving skin protected and revitalized.

Men's Rebalancing

A treatment designed with men's skin in mind to deep clean, refresh, and smooth over-worked skin. Lightweight formulas are used to achieve quick results and balanced skin.

Hydrating

Nature's answer for dehydrated skin that tends to feel dry & tight after cleansing. Black currant oil, hyaluronic acid and humectants bind water into skin cells. Formulas can be lightweight or heavier to accommodate your skin type.

Clarifying

Designed for Acneic complexions. This gentle, yet effective treatment exfoliates and targets acne without stripping or purging, creating a soft, matte finish. Alpine willow and rosemary help reduce inflammation and bacteria on the skin's surface.

Reduce the appearance of fine lines, UV damage, redness, and scarring, with vitamin C and plant stem cell infusions. Alpha-hydroxy acids smooth and refine the skin's texture, while providing a burst of youthful radiance.
